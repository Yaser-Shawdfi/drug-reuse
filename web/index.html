COVID-19 Case Study

How AI Discovered Baricitinib

A step-by-step walkthrough of how BenevolentAI used knowledge graphs to identify an arthritis drug as a COVID-19 treatment — in January 2020, just weeks after the outbreak.

🦠
Dec 31, 2019

Unknown Pneumonia Reported

WHO notified of pneumonia cases of unknown cause in Wuhan, China. No treatment exists. Scientists begin urgently searching for solutions.

🔬
Jan 2020

Genome Sequenced

SARS-CoV-2 genome published. BenevolentAI immediately begins querying their knowledge graph of 1B+ biomedical facts to find drug candidates.

🕸️
Feb 2020

Knowledge Graph Analysis

AI identifies that SARS-CoV-2 uses AAK1 kinase to enter cells. AAK1 is inhibited by Baricitinib — a JAK inhibitor used for rheumatoid arthritis since 2018.

COVID-19 → uses AAK1 for cell entry
AAK1 → inhibited by Baricitinib
∴ Baricitinib → candidate for COVID-19
📄
Feb 4, 2020

Published in The Lancet

BenevolentAI publishes findings in The Lancet Infectious Diseases, proposing Baricitinib as a COVID-19 candidate — one of the earliest AI-driven repurposing publications.

🏥
2020–2021

Clinical Trials (ACTT-2)

NIH-sponsored ACTT-2 trial with 1,033 patients shows Baricitinib + Remdesivir reduces hospital stay by 1 day and improves outcomes in severe patients.

✅
Nov 19, 2021

FDA Full Approval

FDA grants full approval for Baricitinib (Olumiant) for hospitalized COVID-19 patients requiring oxygen. A drug reuse success story — made possible by AI knowledge graphs.

Methodology & References

🎯 Problem Statement

COVID-19 caused over 7 million deaths worldwide. Developing new antiviral drugs from scratch takes 10–15 years and billions of dollars. AI-driven drug reuse offers a faster, cheaper alternative by identifying existing approved drugs that could target SARS-CoV-2.

⚙️ Technical Approach

This project implements a simplified drug reuse pipeline using: (1) curated biomedical datasets, (2) drug feature vector similarity via cosine distance, (3) a knowledge graph with 50+ nodes, and (4) a composite weighted scoring model validated against known reused drugs.

✅ Validation

Known repurposed drugs — Baricitinib (score: 0.94), Dexamethasone (0.92), Remdesivir (0.91) — rank in the top 3 positions, validating the scoring model against real-world clinical outcomes.
